Privacy & Encryption Commitment

Privacy Policy for Loop

At Loop, we believe privacy is a fundamental human right. This Privacy Policy details how we handle your personal information and how our zero-knowledge end-to-end encryption safeguards your communications.

1

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to deliver real-time messaging:

  • Account Profile: Display name, chosen username, email address, and profile photo provided via Google OAuth.
  • Messaging Metadata: Conversation rosters, timestamps of sent messages, and online presence indicators.
  • Encrypted Payload Data: Ciphertext of your messages and encrypted key backups. We cannot read or decrypt the contents of your messages.
2

End-to-End Encryption Architecture

Loop uses modern industry-standard cryptographic algorithms to encrypt all direct and group chat messages locally on your device before they are transmitted over the network.

Because encryption keys are generated and stored locally on your device (and protected by your recovery passphrase), our servers operate under a zero-knowledge model. Neither Loop engineers nor third parties can view your plaintext conversation content.

3

How We Use Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your account and maintain active sessions.
  • Deliver encrypted messages to recipients in real time.
  • Facilitate user search, contacts roster, and group membership.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, and security vulnerabilities.

We do NOT sell, rent, or monetize your personal information or metadata to advertisers or data brokers.

4

Data Storage & Retention

Your account data and encrypted messages are securely hosted using cloud database infrastructure (Google Cloud & Firebase Services) with strict access controls and encryption at rest.

You have the right to clear your message history or request full account deletion at any time from your account settings.

5

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have rights to:

  • Access the personal profile data associated with your account.
  • Update or correct your username and profile information.
  • Export or permanently delete your messaging account and history.
  • Manage your last-seen status and online presence visibility.
6

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our encryption protocols or legal obligations. We will notify users of any material changes through an update banner within the app.
